Paragliding Near Kuala Lumpur

Join us for a flight near Kuala Lumpur with one of our licensed and insured pilots. We fly 30 minutes from Kuala Lumpur at Bukit Jugra or Kuala Kubu Bharu. We choose the best location a few days prior to the scheduled flight based on the weather conditions. You can book for any day or time (during daylight hours) you like. Directions, other activities, and all other details are listed below.

Bukit Jugra:

  • Price: RM240 for the flight and video
  • Flight: 5-8 minutes. Passenger insurance is covered.
  • Deposit: A RM 40 deposit is required for the reservation. The remaining RM 200 can be paid in cash at the paragliding site.
  • Weight: The weight limit here is 85kg (guests over this weight will have to pay RM 50 on site)

Bukit Bubus:

  • Price: RM 250 for the flight and video
  • Flight: 5-10 minutes. 300m above sea level, you will get to enjoy views of Besut's beaches, sea, hills, and nearby islands such as Pulau Perhentian.Passenger insurance is covered.
  • Deposit: A RM 50 deposit is required for the reservation. The remaining RM 200 can be paid in cash at the paragliding site.
  • Weight: The weight limit here is 85kg (guests over this weight will have to pay RM 50 on site)

Kuala Kubu Bharu:

  • Price: RM350 for the flight and video
  • Flight: 6-10 minutes. Higher Mountain than Bukit Jugra. Passenger insurance is covered.
  • Deposit: A RM 53 deposit is required for the reservation. The remaining RM 297 can be paid in cash at the paragliding site.
  • Weight: The weight limit here is 88kg (guests over this weight will have to pay RM 50 on site)
